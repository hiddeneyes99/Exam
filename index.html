Self Management Skills ka matlab hota hai apne aap ko manage karna, taaki tum apne goals ache se achieve kar sako. Yeh skills har field mein zaroori hote hain.

  • 1. Time Management: Apna samay sahi tarike se use karna. Ismein timetable banana, priorities set karna, aur distractions se door rehna zaroori hai.
  • 2. Stress Management: Tension se door rehne ke liye meditation, exercise, ya apne favorite hobby mein time do. Stress se productivity kam hoti hai.
  • 3. Goal Setting: Apne goals ko SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) bana kar set karo taaki tumhe pata ho ki tumhe kya achieve karna hai.
  • 4. Self-Motivation: Apne aap ko encourage karna bahut zaroori hai. Achievements ko celebrate karo aur positive soch rakho! ðŸŒŸ
  • 5. Personal Hygiene: Apna dhyan rakho jaise roz nahaana, haath dhona, aur saaf kapde pehna taaki tum fresh aur healthy raho.
  • 6. Adaptability: Har situation mein adjust karna seekho. Change ko accept karne se life easy ho jaati hai.
